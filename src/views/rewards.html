The Caribbean Sprinter Rewards Program is designed for frequent travelers to accumulate points for each segment traveled. To accumulate these, you must be enrolled in the Rewards Program, which you can do by contacting customer service or speaking to the ticketing agents at any of the terminals.

BONUS POINTS
The Caribbean Sprinter Rewards Program now offers a 25% points bonus for all passengers who travel more than 5 segments within the same calendar month. The bonus points are earned on all points accumulated within the month more than 5 segments were traveled. A segment consists of an origin and destination. For example, if you travel round trip between Belize City and San Pedro, you traveled 2 segments. Therefore, if a passenger who is enrolled in the Sprinter Points program travels at least twice on round-trip itineraries and once on a one-way itinerary within the same month, that passenger will receive the 25% bonus points for all points earned during that month. Bonus points will be credited to rider accounts on the first day of the following month.

The Bonus Points Program replaces the legacy membership pricing. Moving forward all passengers will pay the same fares. It is important to ensure you are enrolled in the Points Program. You can enroll in person or by contacting customer service online through the chat feature on our website, www.sprinter.bz, or by messaging us on Facebook or WhatsApp. The Bonus Points Program creates a landscape where passengers earn their discount, and it allows for a fairer distribution of rewards for our frequent travelers. This allows for the member price or better price to be earned without implementing a monthly membership fee.

Students are also eligible for Bonus Points unless they are on a monthly sailing pass.

If you have any questions, please reach out to our customer service department.
