Preregistration: Caribbean Sprinter's faster and easier option! * Preregistering each passenger in your group will save you time waiting in line at the terminal to purchase tickets
IMPORTANT INFORMATION
The Port Authority requires certain passenger information that must be collected for disclosure to them; the Port Authority maintains no published data protection guidelines, priciples or protocols.
For passengers with disabilities needing assistance with the preregistration process, please reach out to customer service and they will be happy to assist.
After completing preregistration, you will receive a code used by a ticket agent to pre-popuilate and confirm your data. Preregistration is only necessary once. Subsequent sailings will reference your Passenger Profile Data on file.
