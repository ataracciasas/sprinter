You could dive into its depths or snorkel around its edge, and still, some people say there’s not really much to see as the bottom -anyway- is pitch dark… Though, that doesn’t mean the Great Blue Hole isn’t a marvelous place to visit! Our suggestion? Do it from above, from the air! That’s right. You can book a scenic flight-tour with Tropic Air, departing on Tuesdays and Thursdays from San Pedro Caye (at 11 a.m.), Caye Caulker (at 11 a.m.), and Belize City Municipal (at 10 a.m.). And on Sundays from Sand Pedro and Caye Caulker (at 1 p.m.). Or you could also book a private charter tour with 3 and 11 seat aircrafts available. Just make sure to book your San Pedro Caye and Caye Caulker tickets with Caribbean Sprinter!
