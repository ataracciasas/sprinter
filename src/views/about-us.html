Welcome to Caribbean
Sprinter!
We are Belize's best-in-class ferry service, offering multiple daily sailings between Belize City, Caye Caulker, San Pedro Town, and Ambergris Caye. Hop aboard Belize's fastest water taxi and see the difference! Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, as a local or a visitor, you know less time in transit means more time to get things done! Save about an hour on a Belize City to San Pedro round-trip sailing (versus the legacy carrier in town).
Caribbean Sprinter sails boats with a capacity ranging from 60 to 110 passengers and all vessels have at least two motors and are designed with V-shaped hulls to ensure the smoothest ride possible under any given conditions. Mornings are typically the smoothest sailings, but even in choppy waters, Caribbean Sprinter ensures a comfortable ride. For passengers prone to seasickness, seats are available on top decks of select vessels with open air and a clear view of the horizon. Land masses are visible throughout the sailing.
